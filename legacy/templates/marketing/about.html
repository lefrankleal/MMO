real-time fantasy sports

The hottest fantasy sports community

SleeperBot launched in August 2010 as a Fantasy Football Tiersheet application.

SleeperBot immediately expanded to become a discussion community with the release of real-time threaded topics - an innovation in online conversation user interface design.

The online conversation application was an immediate hit on Game Days, crowdsourcing real-time information among members. Eventually, users began visiting on weekdays as well.

SleeperBot expanded into Basketball soon after with Basketball tiersheets, which was featured on many blogs and forums.

Today, SleeperBot is doubling in size each week, adding community features as well as analysis and data tools to help fantasy sports players everywhere have a better understanding of the game and dominate their leagues.
